Career Fair Visit

Career fairs are great places to learn about available careers and to look for summer internships. Even if you plan on teaching or going to graduate school, it is important to be aware of all of your options!

Upcoming Career Fairs

Cal Poly has an upcoming Career Fairs on April 13 and April 14. You must attend the Career Fair and speak with at least one employer (hopefully more!). Visit the Career Services Web site early and register so that you don't miss the fair!

Even though some employers may not specifically state they are looking for mathematics majors, do not let that deter you from going after interesting jobs! What employer wouldn't want an intelligent, hard-working mathematics major who has the ability to quickly learn and clearly communicate technical ideas?
