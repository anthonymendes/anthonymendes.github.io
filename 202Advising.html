Advising Advice

What is advising?

Advising is where you can find answers to questions such as:

  • What courses should I take and when should I take them?
  • How close am I to graduation?
  • Will community college course X count for course Y?
  • How can I best plan for study abroad?
  • What are difficult courses that I should not take concurrently?
  • What do I do if a course that I need is full?
  • How do I declare a concentration or minor, etc? How do I navigate the university bureaucracy?
  • How do I get into a top graduate school?
  • What are the best courses to take for a future high school teacher? Graduate school? Data Science?
  • Which set of courses can I take for the Applied Concentration?
