Mathematics Minor

The Mathematics minor is a wonderful complement to many majors at Cal Poly!

Minor information

The Cal Poly catalog gives the Mathematics minor requirements, which are also outlined on the Mathematics department website. The minor requires you to take

  1. three required lower division courses, and
  2. a choice of four upper division courses with at least two classes at the 400 level.

There is a lot of choice in selecting you four upper division classes. However, watch out for two gotchas:

  1. Upper division courses all have prerequisites which should be checked! The suggested order of mathematics courses has the prerequisites for each course. This information is also found in catalog and the expanded course outlines.
