The Mathematics Minor

The Mathematics minor is a wonderful complement to many majors at Cal Poly! The minor is often only a few extra courses for many math heavy majors.

Minor information

The Cal Poly catalog gives the minor requirements, which are also outlined on the Mathematics department website.

The minor contains three required lower division courses and four upper division courses with at least two at the 400 level. There is a broad range of courses to choose from when selecting the upper division classes. However, watch out for two gotchas:

  1. Upper division courses all have prerequisites which should be checked!

    The suggested order of mathematics courses has the prerequisites for each course. This information is also found in catalog and the expanded course outlines.

  2. Most upper division mathematics courses are only offered once or twice a year, so consult the planned course offerings.

It is not a problem if you decide to change courses later; simply submit a revised Mathematics Minor Application.

How to sign up

After selecting your courses, fill out the Mathematics Minor Application. Submitting the application will automatically send it to the Mathematics Minor Coordinator (Dr. Tony Mendes). You will be emailed when it is submitted to the registrar for processing. It may take a few weeks for the minor to show up in the portal in your degree progress report.
