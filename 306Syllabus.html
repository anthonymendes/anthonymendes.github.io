Content and learning objectives

The content in this course is wonderful! We will cover the content found in the first 5 chapters of the text (at least). The official department course outline contains more details.

Linear algebra is a powerful tool used in almost every subject that uses mathematics. Additionally, this course will greatly enhance the capacity for abstract reasoning. Time spent working on this course will be handsomely rewarded.

Warning: Those students who do not have much experience with upper-division (proof based) mathematics courses may find this course difficult at first, but I will be with you every step of the way. This course will take consistent study and discipline!
