Blue Book Exercises

Blue book exercises will be updated here after they are given in class. The blue books will be collected at the final exam.

  1. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \cos^2 x \sin x \, dx\).
  2. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int_1^2 \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{1+x^2}} \, dx\).
  3. Find the area of a pyramid with side length \(1-x\) on \([0,1]\).
  4. Find the volume of the solid created by spinning the region between \(x^2\) and \(\sqrt{x}\) on \([0,1]\) around the \(x\) axis.
  5. Find the volume of the solid created by spinning the graph of \(y = 1/(1+x^2)^2\) around the \(y\) axis.
  6. Find the arclength of \(y = x^{3/2}\) on \([0,1]\).
  7. Find the surface area of the solid when \(x^3\) on \([0,1]\) is spun around the \(x\) axis.
  8. Find the volume of the solid when \(1/x\) on \([0,1]\) is spun around the \(x\) axis.
  9. Find the work needed to pump gasoline (with density about \(720kg/m^3\)) out of a cylindrical tank oriented horizontally with radius \(1/2m\) and length \(4m\)?
  10. Let \(f(x) = \displaystyle \int_0^x \frac{1}{1 + t^2} \, dt\). Why does the inverse fuction exist? Show that \(g'(x) = 1 + g(x)^2\).
  11. Evaluate \(\displaystyle\frac{d}{dx} \sqrt{\ln (2 x + 1)}\).
  12. Find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \ln (1 + \frac{1}{x})\).
  13. Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int (x^2 + 2 x + 1)/x \, dx\).
  14. Solve \(\ln (x - 1) = 4 - \ln (x + 1)\).
  15. Differentiate \(1/(1-e^{-x})\).
  16. Find the volume of the solid created by rotating \(e^x\) on \([0,\ln 3]\) around the \(x\) axis.
  17. Find the value of \(x > 0\) that maximizes \(x^{-x}\).
  18. If \(P'(t) = k P(t)\), \(P(0) = 1000\), and \(P(1) = 1050\), find when \(P(t) = 2000\).
  19. Differentiate \(\arcsin \sqrt{x}\) and integrate \(\int 2/\sqrt{1-4x^2} \, dx\).
  20. Differentiate \(x^{\arctan x}\) and integrate \(\int 2 x/(1+x^4) \, dx\).
  21. Integrate \(\int 2 e^x \cosh x \, dx\).
  22. Integrate \(\int (x+1)^2 e^{-2 x} \, dx\).
  23. Integrate \(\int e^{2 x} \sin (3 x) \, dx\).
  24. Integrate \(\int \arcsin x \, dx\).
  25. Integrate \(\int \sin^3 x \cos^6 x \, dx\).
  26. Integrate \(\int \sin^4 (x/4) \, dx\).
  27. Integrate \(\int x^3/\sqrt{1 - x^2} \, dx\).
  28. Integrate \(\int \frac{2 x + 1}{x (3 x + 1)} \, dx\).
  29. Integrate \(\int \frac{2x^2}{x^2-1} \, dx\).
  30. Approximate \(\int_0^1 \sqrt{1 + x^3} \, dx\) with Simpson's rule with \(n = 8\).
  31. Evaluate \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(x^2 + 1)}{x}\) and \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\arctan x}{\arcsin x}\).
