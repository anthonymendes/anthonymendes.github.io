Course content

I have been greatly looking forward to sharing the ideas in this course with you!

The topics are found in chapters 1–4 of our textbook. These are extremely useful topics which find numerous applications in a wide variety of subjects. The official department course outline contains more details.

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to

  1. Take limits and be able to classify functions as continuous or discontinuous.
  2. Differentiate functions of one variable and study applications of derivatives, including finding tangent lines, solving for maximums and minimums, and solving related rates problems.
  3. Integrate and describe the relationship between the integral and the derivative.
