Content

We will be studying the representation theory of the symmetric group. This topic combines combinatorics, abstract algebra, and linear algebra to understand how representations of the symmetric group break into irreducible components. Students who complete this course will be well prepared to enter a PhD program in algebraic combinatorics.

The material in this course is outstanding, maybe even better than the content last quarter! I have written notes for this course. The content of this course is the content of the notes.

References

We will be following our course notes closely. Excellent second references include

  1. The Symmetric Group: Representations, Combinatorial Algorithms, and Symmetric Functions by Bruce E. Sagan. This is a wonderful book!

  2. The Representation theory of the Symmetric groups by G. D. James. This is an older reference, but still quite good.

    If these two references are not sufficient for you, then ask me and I can recommend more texts on the subject.

Exercise Sets

The exercise sets are important and high quality work is expected. You will not receive credit for an exercise unless work is included. Please turn in assignments of a quality appropriate for a professional workplace.

Please get started on the exercises early! To keep everyone on track, late assignments will be penalized 20% per day late. If life issues get in the way, please let me know before the assignment is due so that we can make special arrangements.

Follow these steps when turning in the assignments and exams:

  1. Neatly and clearly write out the solutions by hand. This may require writing rough drafts; please do not submit messy scratch work. If you are using a tablet to write out solutions or know mathematical typesetting (LaTeX) or have any other method of creating legible solutions in a .pdf file, then of course this can be used instead of handwritten solutions.
  2. It is difficult to read and grade .jpg image files, so please create a single .pdf file from your exercise sets using a document scanner phone application.
  3. Upload the file to in our Canvas site before the due date.
