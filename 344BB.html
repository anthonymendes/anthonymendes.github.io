Blue Book Exercises

Blue book exercises will be updated here after they are given in class.

  1. Find \(\displaystyle\mathcal{L} \left[ 1 + 2 \sin^2(3 t) + t^4 \right]\).
  2. Find \(\displaystyle\mathcal{L}^{-1} \left[ \frac{4 s - 2}{s^2+4} + \frac{4}{s} \right]\).
  3. Using the Laplace transform, solve \(\begin{cases} y'' - y' = -t \\ y(0) = 0, y'(0) = 1. \end{cases}\)
  4. Find \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left[ e^{-2 t} \cos 4 t \right]\) and \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L}^{-1} \left[ \frac{s}{(s+2)^2 + 9} \right]\).
  5. Find \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left[ u_{2 \pi}(t) \sin 2 t \right]\) and \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L}^{-1} \left[ e^{-3 s}/(s^2 + 4) \right]\)
  6. Solve \(y'(t) = g(t)\) with \(y(0) = 1\) where \(g(t)\) is the function which is \(2-t\) for \(t < 2\) and \(0\) otherwise.
  7. Solve \(\displaystyle \begin{cases} y' - y = \delta(t - 3), \\ y(0) = 24. \end{cases}\)
  8. Find \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L}^{-1} \left[ \dfrac{1}{s+2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{s+1} \right]\) using convolution.
  9. Solve \(x^2 y'' - x y' - 3 y = 0\).
  10. Solve \(x^2 y'' + 3 x y' + 5 y = 0\).
  11. Solve \(y' + 6 y = e^{-2x}\) using the integrating factor.
  12. Solve \(y'' - x y' - y = 0\).
  13. Solve \(2 x^2 y'' - x y' + (1 - x)y = 0\).
  14. Find the third Legendre polynomial \(p_3(x)\) by solving the Legendre equation when \(k = 3\).
  15. Suppose \(x^4 = a_0 p_0(x) + a_1 p_1(x) + a_2 p_2(x) + \cdots\) where \(p_k(x)\) is the \(k\) th Legendre polynomial. What is \(a_2\)?
  16. Let \(\displaystyle v_1 = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}\), \(\displaystyle v_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ -2 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\), \(\displaystyle v_3 = \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 2 \\ 5\end{bmatrix}\). Find constants \(a_1, a_2, a_3\) such that \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}\) is equal to \(a_1 v_1 + a_2 v_2 + a_3 v_3\).
  17. Find the vector in the span of \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}3 \\ -1 \\ -1\end{bmatrix}\) and \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 0 \\ 3\end{bmatrix}\) closest to \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\).
  18. Find an orthogonal basis for the span of the vectors \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\), \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}3 \\ 1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\), \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}0 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\).
  19. Which line \(f(x) = a x + b\) best fits the data \(\{(0,0),(1,1),(2,0)\}\)?
  20. Use the projection matrix to find the vector in the span of \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}\), \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}\) closest to \(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix}a \\ b \\ c \\ d\end{bmatrix}\).
  21. Use the normal equation to find the line \(f(x) = a x + b\) that fits \(\{(0,0),(-1,1),(1,2)\}\).
  22. Which function of the form \(a + b \cos x\) is closest to \(x\) on \([0,\pi]\)?
  23. Suppose \(y_k'' = - k y_k\) and \(y_m'' = - m y_m\) and both \(y_k\) and \(y_m\) satisfy \(y(-L) = y(L)\) and \(y'(-L) = y'(L)\). Show that \(\langle y_k, y_m \rangle\) on \(PS[-L,L]\).
  24. Find the Fourier transform of \(u_0(t) e^{-t}\).
  25. a. Find the Fourier transform for the Dirac delta function. b. What is \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{i \omega t} \, d\omega\)?
  26. Solve the differential equation \(y''(t) = - \omega^2 y(t)\) with the initial conditions \(y(0) = 0\) and \(y'(0) = 0\).
