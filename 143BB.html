Blue Book Exercises

Blue book exercises will be updated here after they are given in class. These exercises will be collected on the last day of class.

  1. Find the degree \(3\) Taylor polynomial for \(\ln (1 + x)\) at \(x = 0\).
  2. Find the degree \(n\) Taylor polynomial for \(1/(3 + x)\) at \(x = 0\).
  3. The degree \(5\) Taylor polynomial for \(\sin x\) is \(x - x^3/3! + x^5/5!\). Find a bound on the error when using this to approximate \(\sin 2\).
