Who are you?

I would like to get to know you so that I can better serve you! Please upload your answers the following questions on Canvas by Apr 2.

  1. Why did you become a mathematics major? What do you enjoy about mathematics? Do you like proofs?
  2. What ideas do you have about your future career (it is perfectly fine if you don’t have a solid plan yet)?
  3. If you are planning on teaching, what level do you want to teach?
  4. Do you plan to attend graduate or professional school right after getting your Bachelor’s degree or are you planning on starting a career? What are your current plans?
  5. What are your thoughts on which concentration you will pursue (if any) as a math major (if you don’t know yet, this course might help you decide!)?
  6. Are you planning to minor or double major? If so, in what? This courses can to help you learn about common minors for math majors.
  7. Are you interested in a career where a significant component of the job is coding or otherwise working with computers? Nearly all careers require some computer work, but if you are particularly interested in such a job or alternately are hoping to minimize computer work, state that here.
  8. Do you have significant interests or obligations outside math (sports, band, double major, study abroad, working long hours, childcare) that could make scheduling courses difficult?
  9. Which amazing opportunities most appeal to you?
