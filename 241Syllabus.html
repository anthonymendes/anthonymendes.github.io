Course content

I have been greatly looking forward to sharing the ideas in this course with you!

The topics are found in chapters 14–16 of our textbook. These are extremely useful topics which find numerous applications in a wide variety of subjects. The official department course outline contains more details.

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to

  1. Do partial differentiation and study applications such as tangent planes, maximum/minimum problems with and without constraints.
  2. Do multiple integration and/or applications such as volumes, surface area and moments.
  3. Work with vector fields and vector integral theorems.
