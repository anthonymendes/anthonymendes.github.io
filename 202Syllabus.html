Course content

This content of this course is designed to help you make the most out of your time as a Cal Poly Mathematics major! We will discuss

  • What the mathematics major is
  • Curriculum choices and planning
  • Graduate school in pure and applied mathematics
  • Mathematics education and the teaching credential
  • Minors that pair well with the mathematics major
  • Advising resources
  • Careers and internships in mathematics and related fields
  • Research opportunities in mathematics majors

This course is graded CR/NC (credit/no credit). To earn Credit, you must attend our class meetings (missing no more than 1 non-in-class-advising meeting) and complete all assignments.
