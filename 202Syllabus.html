Course content

This content of this course is designed to help you make the most out of the mathematics major! In particular, this course will introduce you to

  • The general curriculum in mathematics
  • The applied, pure, and teaching concentrations
  • Graduate school in pure and applied mathematics
  • Graduate school in mathematics education and the teaching credential
  • Minors that pair well with the mathematics major
  • Advising resources
  • Careers and internships in mathematics and related fields
  • Research opportunities in mathematics majors
  • Work opportunities for mathematics majors

The faculty at Cal Poly (and especially the mathematics professors) truly care about you and love helping you get the next stage of your life. I am looking forward to working with you in this course!
