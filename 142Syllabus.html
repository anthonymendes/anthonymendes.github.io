Textbook

The Cal Poly Calculus text is Calculus, 8th ed by James Stewart.

The text will be used as a reference to augment the lectures and exercises. At times we may not be following the text exactly. This text will also be used in the remainder of the Cal Poly Calculus sequence.

Course content

The topics in this course include differentiation and integration of elementary transcendental functions. Along the way we will better understand applications of integration, including areas, volumes, work, arc length, lateral surface area, and center of mass. These are extremely useful topics which find numerous applications in a wide variety of subjects. The official department course outline contains more details.
