Textbook

Our textbook is Linear Algebra and its applications, 4th edition by David C. Lay. This is not the most recent edition of the textbook, so you should be able to buy an inexpensive used copy online. It is also available at the bookstore.

Content

I have been greatly looking forward to sharing the ideas in this course with you! The topics in the course are found in the first six chapters of the textbook. These are extremely useful topics which find numerous applications in a wide variety of subjects. The official department course outline contains more details.

Blue Book exercises 5%
Group quizzes 10%

Midterm exams 40%
