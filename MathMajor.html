Major requirements

The Mathematics Major is achieved when all Core requirements are satisfied and one of the following tracks is satisfied:

  • General Track: 7 classes from List A.
  • Applied Track: 3 classes from List B and 4 classes from List A.
  • Teaching Track: 4 classes from List C and 3 classes from List A.

Classes in tracks must contain at 3 classes at the 4000+ level, 5 classes with a MATH prefix, and courses cannot appear elsewhere in the major.
